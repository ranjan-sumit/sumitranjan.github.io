About Me

AI Leader & Researcher

Driving Innovation in AI Systems

PhD Scholar

AgenticAI Security at BITS Pilani

I'm Sumit Ranjan — AI researcher, author, and security strategist with a decade of experience at the forefront of AI innovation and safety. My focus lies in building secure, scalable, and ethical AI systems with deep specialization in Agentic AI, LLM security, and AI governance.

Currently, I serve as the Head of Responsible AI at Forcespot, leading cross-functional initiatives to integrate ethics, transparency, and risk mitigation into enterprise AI deployments. Alongside, I'm pursuing a PhD in Agentic AI Security at BITS Pilani, researching trustworthy agent-based systems.

I contribute to the OWASP Agentic Security Initiative, helping define global standards in GenAI safety. My past roles span healthcare, retail, and finance—delivering real-world impact by translating complex models into meaningful business outcomes.

As an author, I've written the bestselling book "Applied Deep Learning and Computer Vision for Self-Driving Cars" and am currently working on "Enterprise Agentic AI" which explores secure deployment of autonomous AI systems in enterprises.

Professional Profile

I'm Sumit Ranjan — a seasoned AI researcher, author, and security strategist with over a decade of experience driving innovation at the intersection of AI development and AI security. I specialize in building secure, scalable, and responsible AI systems using state-of-the-art models like GPT-4, LLaMA, and Falcon, with deep expertise in agentic AI, LLM safety, and AI governance.

Currently, I serve as Head of Responsible AI at Forcespot, where I lead cross-functional initiatives to embed ethics, transparency, and risk mitigation into enterprise AI adoption. I'm also a PhD scholar in Responsible Generative AI at BITS Pilani, researching how to build trustworthy agent-based systems.

As a contributor to the OWASP Agentic Security Initiative, I'm actively shaping global standards in AI safety. I've led high-impact AI initiatives across global enterprises in healthcare, retail, and finance—translating complex models into real-world value and authored the bestselling book, "Applied Deep Learning and Computer Vision for Self-Driving Cars". My upcoming book, "Enterprise Agentic AI", explores the secure deployment of autonomous AI systems in modern enterprises.

At the core, I'm driven by one mission: to build AI that's not just powerful, but also safe, ethical, and accountable.

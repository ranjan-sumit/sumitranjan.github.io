About

Builder first. Researcher always.

I'm Sumit Ranjan — an AI architect and researcher with 12+ years designing and shipping production AI systems across healthcare, retail, finance, automotive, and semiconductor. Today my focus is narrow and deliberate: making agentic AI systems reliable, secure, and cheaper to run.

That means building compact Small Language Models (SLMs) that are domain-trained and verifiable, hardening LLM and voice pipelines against real adversarial attacks, and shaping the governance standards enterprises will depend on. My PhD research at BITS Pilani sits exactly at this intersection — how autonomous agents fail, how they're exploited, and how to build them so they don't.

"The most useful AI isn't the largest model — it's the one you can trust, afford, and explain."

I contribute to the OWASP Agentic AI security standards, authored Enterprise Agentic AI (Apress/Springer) and a bestselling book on self-driving cars (Packt), and teach agentic AI at universities across the UAE and India. I advise startups and enterprises as a fractional architect, turning AI proofs-of-concept into production systems that hold up under scrutiny.
